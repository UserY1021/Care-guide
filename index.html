Care Guide

To preserve the appearance, color, texture, and overall quality of this garment, always follow the care instructions provided on the label. Machine wash separately or with similar colors using cold water or a maximum temperature of 30°C (86°F) and a mild detergent. Avoid using bleach, chlorine, fabric softeners containing harsh chemicals, or abrasive cleaning agents, as these products may damage the fibers, affect the finish, or cause premature fading.

Dark or intensely dyed fabrics may release excess color during the first several washes. It is strongly recommended to wash this garment separately before first use and to avoid contact with light-colored clothing, upholstery, handbags, shoes, or other fabrics while the garment is damp or exposed to excessive moisture. Color transfer may occur due to friction, humidity, perspiration, or prolonged contact with wet surfaces.

Do not tumble dry. Excessive heat may cause shrinkage, distortion, damage to the fabric structure, or deterioration of the lining and decorative elements. After washing, gently reshape the garment while damp and hang it to dry naturally in a well-ventilated area away from direct sunlight. Extended exposure to sunlight may gradually fade the color and reduce the original appearance of the fabric.

Do not wring, twist, or excessively stretch the garment when wet, as this may permanently affect its shape. If ironing is necessary, use a low-temperature setting and iron only on the reverse side or place a protective cloth between the iron and the fabric. Do not iron directly over prints, decorative details, embroidery, labels, zippers, buttons, or any ornamental components.

Avoid prolonged contact with perfumes, cosmetics, lotions, oils, deodorants, alcohol-based products, solvents, cleaning chemicals, paints, adhesives, or any corrosive substances, as they may stain, discolor, or permanently damage the fabric. Keep away from rough surfaces, sharp objects, hooks, Velcro fasteners, pet claws, or abrasive materials that could snag or tear the fibers.

This garment is intended for everyday wear under normal conditions. It is not designed for exposure to open flames, sparks, high temperatures, industrial environments, extreme outdoor conditions, or activities that involve excessive abrasion. Do not leave the garment inside a closed vehicle under direct sunlight for extended periods or store it while damp, folded tightly, or compressed for long periods of time.

Minor variations in color, texture, stitching, or finish are natural characteristics of the manufacturing process and should not be considered defects. Normal wear, repeated washing, and regular use may gradually alter the appearance of the garment over time.

For best results, store the garment clean, completely dry, and on a suitable hanger to help maintain its original shape. Do not use this garment as a blanket, pillow, cleaning cloth, carrying bag, pet bedding, picnic mat, or for any purpose other than that for which it was designed. While these recommendations may seem obvious, following them will help maximize the lifespan and appearance of the product.

The manufacturer shall not be held responsible for damage resulting from improper washing, failure to follow care instructions, use of unauthorized cleaning products, alterations performed by third parties, accidental misuse, or normal wear and tear resulting from regular use.
